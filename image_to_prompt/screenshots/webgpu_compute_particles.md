# Three.js Scene Description

## Overall Composition
This is a striking perspective-driven visualization featuring a vast receding grid of spheres that creates a powerful sense of depth and vanishing point convergence toward the horizon. The geometric precision and mathematical regularity suggest a data visualization or procedural art piece.

## Geometry & Structure
- **Primary Elements**: Thousands of small spheres arranged in a perfectly uniform orthogonal grid
- **Grid Layout**: Regular rows and columns extending into the distance with mathematical precision
- **Perspective**: Classic one-point perspective with the vanishing point centered on the horizon, creating dramatic depth
- **Scale**: The spheres maintain consistent size in world space, emphasizing the perspective compression as they recede

## Lighting & Shading
- **Ambient Illumination**: Soft, even lighting that reveals the volumetric nature of the spheres
- **Surface Quality**: Matte to semi-reflective spheres with subtle shading gradients
- **Shadow & Depth**: Gentle shadowing enhances the three-dimensional quality and ground plane definition

## Color Palette
- **Primary Color Range**: Vibrant lime green to chartreuse green
- **Secondary Accent**: Deep red/crimson elements scattered throughout, creating visual rhythm and data emphasis points
- **Color Distribution**: The red spheres appear to mark specific nodes or data points within the grid, possibly indicating anomalies, selections, or significance
- **Contrast**: The warm red against cool green creates visual tension and focal interest

## Atmospheric & Technical Properties
- **Depth of Field**: Slight blur or atmospheric perspective on distant elements
- **Rendering Style**: Clean, mathematically precise polygon rendering
- **Background**: The receding grid merges with a neutral background at the horizon
- **Resolution**: Sharp, detailed rendering suggesting high-quality three.js WebGL implementation
