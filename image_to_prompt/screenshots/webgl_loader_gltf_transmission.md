# 3D Scene Description: Decorative Glass Dish with Ornamental Spheres

This is a sophisticated three.js rendered scene featuring a transparent glass vessel with an elegant, organic design floating against a soft, gradient background.

- **Shape**: A distinctive sculptural form combining a shallow, disc-like base with a tall, curved spout or lip that curves upward and inward
- **Material**: Clear, translucent glass with subtle refractive properties
- **Transparency**: The glass demonstrates realistic light transmission and refraction, allowing visibility of contents while maintaining optical depth
- **Base**: A shallow, turquoise or cyan-tinted dish that contrasts with the clear upper portion

- **Material**: The spheres appear metallic or highly polished, with specular highlights indicating realistic PBR (Physically Based Rendering) materials

- **Lighting**: Warm, diffuse illumination with subtle highlights on the glass and spheres
