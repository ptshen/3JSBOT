# 3D Scene Description: Blue Shark Sculpture

This is a striking three.js rendered scene featuring a **bold, abstract blue sculpture** positioned against a minimalist environment. The piece demonstrates excellent lighting and shadow rendering with a professional studio-like setup.

- **Form**: An avant-garde, geometric abstraction of a shark constructed from flowing, angular planes
