# Three.js Scene Description

## Overall Composition
This is a dynamic 3D visualization featuring a scattered field of geometric shapes suspended in white space, creating a sense of depth and motion through the composition.

## Primary Elements

### Geometric Forms
- **Triangles**: The dominant shape type, ranging from small to large scale
- **Quadrilaterals**: Various polygonal shapes interspersed throughout
- **Mixed Polyhedra**: Some compound shapes that appear to be constructed from multiple planes

### Color Palette
- **Primary Color**: Deep crimson/maroon red (#8B1A1A or similar)
- **Secondary Variation**: Brighter scarlet and burgundy tones
- **Accent**: Subtle orange/rust highlights on some surfaces catching the light
- **Background**: Clean white, providing maximum contrast

## Lighting & Shading
- **Material**: Matte to semi-glossy finish with realistic surface reflections
- **Light Direction**: Appears to come from the upper left, creating clear shadow definition on the back and undersides of shapes
- **Depth Perception**: Shadows and perspective lines create strong three-dimensional depth

## Composition & Distribution
- Dense cluster in the center
- Progressive scatter toward edges
- Varied rotation angles suggesting organic, chaotic motion
- Smaller shapes tend toward the periphery
- Larger forms anchor the center composition
