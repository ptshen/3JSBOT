# 3D Scene Description

## Overall Composition
This is a charming European-style courtyard rendered in Three.js, featuring a carefully composed urban plaza with classical architecture surrounding a central open space.

## Architecture

**Left Building:**
- A traditional Italian-style palazzo with a warm ochre/golden-yellow facade
- Four stories tall with regular fenestration pattern
- Rectangular windows with dark frames arranged in a symmetrical grid
- Classical proportions suggesting Renaissance or Baroque influence
- Clean, flat facade with minimal ornamentation

**Right Side Structures:**
- A modern concrete building with a more utilitarian aesthetic
- Neutral beige/gray coloring contrasting with the historical architecture
- Features a geometric pattern of small square windows or ventilation units
- Represents a contemporary addition to the historic environment
- Solid, imposing form creating an urban canyon effect

## Central Feature
- **Large Blue Sphere:** A prominent glossy cobalt-blue spherical object positioned in the center of the plaza
- Perfectly reflective surface catching and reflecting the surrounding environment
- Serves as a striking focal point against the muted earth tones of the architecture
- Creates an interesting juxtaposition of modern art installation against classical setting

## Lighting & Atmosphere
- Warm, golden-hour sunlight illuminating the left facade
- High-contrast shadows creating dramatic depth
- Clear blue sky visible above
- Realistic shadow casting on the ground plane and building surfaces

## Ground Plane
- Paved courtyard floor with subtle texture and shadow details
- Appears to be stone or concrete with age-appropriate weathering

## Style & Context
This scene effectively blends historical European architecture with contemporary minimalist art, creating a thought-provoking modern plaza environment.
