# Three.js Scene Analysis

This is a vibrant, abstract 3D geometric composition rendered in Three.js, featuring a chaotic arrangement of colorful cubic and rectangular forms against a white background. The scene has a distinctly pixelated, low-poly aesthetic with bold, contrasting colors.

- Dominant neon cyan/turquoise forms creating structure
- Black cubic elements and outlines providing contrast
- Bright accent colors: magenta, red, yellow-green, blue, orange, and pink
- White background allowing colors to pop

**Geometric Forms:**
