# 3D Scene Description: Dispersion Visualization

## Overall Composition
This is a sophisticated 3D data visualization rendered in Three.js that demonstrates the concept of **dispersion** or **variance distribution**. The scene features a dynamic, wave-like structure that elegantly transforms from organized to chaotic, visualizing how data spreads across a spectrum of values.

## Key Elements

### Main Structure
- **Central Grid Wall**: A large, vertical plane composed of hundreds of small cubic blocks arranged in a grid pattern
- **Color Gradient**: The blocks transition from **cream/beige** (left/center) to **dark navy/black** (right), creating a visual spectrum
- **Dispersal Effect**: The blocks progressively spread outward from the center, creating a fan or explosion-like pattern that intensifies toward the right side

### Layout & Perspective
- **Checkerboard Floor**: A classic blue and gray checkered plane provides spatial grounding and enhances depth perception
- **Camera Angle**: Slightly elevated, angled view that emphasizes the three-dimensional depth and the dispersal spreading pattern
- **Scale Variation**: Cube sizes appear consistent, but their spatial distribution creates the illusion of increasing disorder

### Labels & Measurement
- **Top Label**: "Dispersion" clearly identifies the visualization's purpose
- **Numerical Scale**: Values visible (0.0, 1.0, 2.0, 3.0, 5.0) indicating measurement ranges or standard deviations
- **Vertical Markers**: Left-side labels (1.0, 2.0, 3.0) suggest quantitative axes
