# Three.js Scene Description

## Overall Composition
This is a vibrant, celebratory particle system rendered in three.js, featuring a dynamic confetti-like effect against a soft, gradient background.

## Background
The backdrop transitions smoothly from a **warm pink on the left** through soft **white in the center** to a pale **yellow-green on the right**, creating a gentle, cheerful atmosphere. The gradient is horizontal and evenly distributed, providing a clean canvas for the foreground elements.

## Particle System
The scene is populated with numerous **small, elongated confetti pieces** scattered throughout the composition:

- **Shape**: Thin, rectangular strips or shredded paper-like particles
- **Color Palette**: Predominantly warm tones including:
  - Magenta and hot pink
  - Red and burgundy
  - Gold and amber
  - Some cooler teal/cyan accents
  
- **Distribution**: Particles are dispersed across the entire frame with varying density, creating a natural, organic spread
- **Scale**: Individual confetti pieces are relatively small, creating a sense of depth and layering
- **Movement Hint**: The randomized positioning suggests mid-animation state, as if particles are falling or floating through space
