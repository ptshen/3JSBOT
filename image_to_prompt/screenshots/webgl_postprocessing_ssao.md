# 3D Scene Description: Abstract Geometric Composition

This is a dynamic, isometric arrangement of colorful three-dimensional geometric shapes rendered in Three.js. The scene features a vibrant, playful collection of polyhedra and angular forms scattered across a neutral gray background, creating a sense of organized chaos with strong visual appeal.

- **Vibrant neons**: Bright cyan/turquoise and lime green

- **Deep accents**: Dark maroon and forest green

1. **Central clusters** of interlocking polyhedra create focal points
2. **Cubes and rectangular solids** in varying scales scattered throughout
3. **Pyramidal and tetrahedral forms** adding angular variety
4. **Irregular polygonal shapes** suggesting randomized or procedurally-generated geometry

The composition uses isometric perspective, with shapes arranged in a loose, organic cluster that feels both chaotic and balanced. Objects overlap naturally, creating layered depth without appearing cluttered.

This style is typical of modern generative art, data visualization, or abstract 3D design applications.
