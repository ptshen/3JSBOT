# Three.js Scene Analysis

- **Soft Diffuse Lighting**: Heavy use of ambient lighting creates a hazy, dreamlike quality
- **Atmospheric Perspective**: Significant depth-of-field or fog effect creates a misty, obscured background
- **Warm Glow**: The overall illumination suggests either sunrise/sunset or a strong warm light source

### Geometry & Terrain
- **Mountainous Forms**: Three distinct rounded mountain or hill-like shapes visible
- **Organic Shapes**: Smooth, undulating surfaces with no sharp edges
- **Layered Depth**: Mountains appear in varying levels of focus, suggesting distance and atmospheric haze
- **Subtle Shadows**: Gentle gradations in tone suggest soft volumetric modeling rather than hard shadows

### Composition
- **Minimalist Design**: Very sparse scene with only essential geometric forms
- **Soft Transitions**: Blurred boundaries between forms and background
- **Centered Arrangement**: Mountains positioned across the frame's middle ground
- **Gradient Sky**: Transitions smoothly from warm peachy tones to subtle variations

- Likely uses simple geometry (spheres or custom meshes)
