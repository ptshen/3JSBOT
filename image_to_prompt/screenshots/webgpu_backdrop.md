# 3D Scene Description

## Overall Composition
This is a vibrant, stylized 3D scene rendered in three.js featuring a central animated character surrounded by a collection of colorful spheres against a deep blue gradient background. The scene has a playful, cartoonish aesthetic with carefully balanced lighting and composition.

## Central Character
- **Design**: A cheerful, low-polygon female character with an orange/reddish complexion
- **Appearance**: Wearing a yellow and white outfit with black accents
- **Pose**: Dynamic stance with arms raised in an expressive, celebratory gesture
- **Details**: Simple geometric features with a friendly expression, including distinctive hair elements on top

## Surrounding Spheres
Arranged organically around the character are approximately 6-7 spheres with varying properties:

1. **Light Blue/Purple sphere** (upper left) - Semi-transparent with a pearl-like quality
2. **Dark Gray/Black sphere** (left-center) - Matte finish with a bright white highlight
3. **Bright Blue sphere** (upper right) - Glossy with a prominent red reflective highlight
4. **Yellow-Green sphere** (center-right) - Highly reflective with warm tones
5. **Brown/Tan sphere** (right) - Textured, matte appearance
6. **Additional spheres** - Varying in reflectivity and color saturation

## Lighting & Atmosphere
- **Background**: Smooth blue gradient (darker at edges, lighter toward center)
- **Lighting**: Three-point lighting setup with warm and cool light sources creating dimensional shadows
- **Reflections**: Spheres display realistic specular highlights and reflections
- **Overall Tone**: Cheerful and inviting with professional material rendering
