# Three.js Scene Description: Motorcycle Showroom

## Overall Composition
This is a meticulously rendered 3D motorcycle showroom scene displaying an impressive fleet of identical bikes arranged in precise, symmetrical rows. The perspective uses a wide-angle lens effect, creating dramatic depth and drawing the viewer's eye through the collection.

## Environment & Lighting
- **Setting**: A modern, upscale showroom with polished wooden flooring
- **Lighting**: Sophisticated ambient and accent lighting with:
  - Recessed ceiling lights creating subtle illumination
  - Strategic spotlighting that highlights the chrome and metallic components of each motorcycle
  - Warm overhead ambient light balanced with cooler accent tones
  - Ambient displays and screens visible in the background (TVs/monitors on walls)

## Motorcycle Details
- **Design**: Cruiser-style or sport-touring motorcycles with distinctive styling
- **Color Scheme**: Primarily white/silver bodies with striking blue accents
- **Materials**: 
  - Highly reflective chrome elements (wheels, handlebars, exhausts)
  - Glossy paint with accurate specular highlights
  - Metallic finishes that catch and reflect the showroom lighting
- **Condition**: All bikes appear pristine and identical, suggesting a coordinated fleet or dealer inventory

## Spatial Arrangement
- Bikes are organized in parallel, converging rows
- Precise alignment and uniform spacing creates visual rhythm
- The vanishing point composition emphasizes scale and quantity
- Creates a sense of organized abundance and showroom prestige

## Technical Quality
- Advanced material shaders with realistic reflectivity
- Accurate shadows and ambient occlusion
- Depth of field effects (slight blur on background elements)
- High polygon count for detailed bike geometry
